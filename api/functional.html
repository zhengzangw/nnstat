nnstat.functional¶

nnstat.functional.trust_ratio(update, weight, p=2, lw=True, pattern=None)[source]¶

reciprocal of the trust ratio defined in Large Batch Training of Convolutional Networks. Here the rust ratio is defined as the ratio between the norm of the update and the norm of the weight.

Parameters:

  • update (StateDict) – the update of the weight

  • weight (StateDict) – the weight

  • p (int, optional) – the norm order. Defaults to 2.

  • lw (bool, optional) – whether to use layer-wise norm. Defaults to True.

  • pattern (Union[None, str], optional) – the pattern to filter the weight. Defaults to None.

Returns:

StateDict – the trust ratio
